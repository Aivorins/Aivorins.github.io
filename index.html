ABOUT AIVORIN

Built for people who
value their attention.

Aivorin is an independent studio building free browser tools. No venture funding, no growth metrics, no dark patterns.

Every extension does one thing and does it well. We believe software should be honest about what it does and invisible when it doesn't need to be.

All tools are free, require no account, and collect zero data. That's the whole deal.

PRINCIPLE 01
Zero data. Zero noise.
Nothing is collected, stored, or shared. Your browser stays yours.
PRINCIPLE 02
Free. Always.
Every tool is free. Support is appreciated but never required to access anything.
PRINCIPLE 03
One tool, one job.
No feature bloat. Each extension solves a single problem and gets out of the way.
