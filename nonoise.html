HOW IT WORKS

Three layers of friction.

NoNoise makes opening social media feel like work — so your impulse to scroll never gets a foothold.

01 · INTERCEPT
Tab Closure
The moment a blocked domain loads in any tab, NoNoise closes it instantly — before the page fully renders or you see a single pixel of content.
02 · COOLDOWN
25-Second Timer
Before the password prompt even appears, a 25-second countdown runs. Impulsive clicks to disable don't get through. You have to really mean it.
03 · PASSWORD
Fresh Password Lock
A new SHA-256 password is required every single activation. It's cleared on deactivation — no memorised bypass, no autopilot. Every session starts deliberate.
